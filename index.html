About Pastor Obed Lucky Ahimbisibwe

Pastor Obed Lucky Ahimbisibwe is a Gospel artist, author, preacher, mentor and founder of Eternal Life Healing and Prophetic International Ministries (ELHPIM). His ministry is dedicated to preaching Jesus Christ, winning souls, healing lives and transforming nations.
